  • Chose this for health and fitness — swam twice weekly for 6 months.
  • Having access to my school’s squad swimming helped me stick with it.
  • Consistency and measurable goals (e.g. number of sessions) made it easy to track progress.
  • Fit naturally into my routine, which made commitment easier.

SIA 2 – Assistant Guide Caving

  • This matched my passion, so the work felt enjoyable rather than forced.
  • Logging cave hours and completing the course was smooth because I was motivated.
  • Feedback from Matt Smith gave me guidance on improving navigation and communication.
  • Reinforced leadership and risk management skills in real-world scenarios.

SIA 3 – Traditional Climbing

  • I had little prior climbing experience — so I did an immersive week-long trip at Mount Arapiles.
  • Learning through immersion worked well and helped me make fast progress.
  • Feedback from Julie Engelhardt helped me improve route reading and gear use.
  • Reinforced the importance of feedback and learning from experts.

Adventurous Journey

Planning the Journey

  • Finding a qualified mentor (Greg Roberts) made the process clearer and more manageable.
  • Mentor feedback helped refine safety documents, route plans, and logistics.
  • Realised the importance of early preparation and accurate documentation.
  • Planning helped me develop detailed thinking and contingency planning.

Leading the Journey

  • Designed the trip to match my group’s abilities — but still faced real-time challenges.
  • Learned to adjust expectations and prioritise meaningful goals when plans shifted.
  • Flexibility was crucial — not everything can go exactly as planned.
  • Leadership meant keeping the group safe, engaged, and motivated.

Reviewing the Journey

  • Ran a feedback session immediately after the trip while details were fresh.
  • Participants shared constructive feedback:
    • Needed earlier communication of plans
    • Some days were too physically demanding
    • Weather warnings were missed
  • I used this feedback to set earlier deadlines, monitor weather more closely, and adjust pacing in future planning.
  • Helped me improve both planning and communication skills.
